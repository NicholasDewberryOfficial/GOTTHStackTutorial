As you might've noticed, we use parseglob a lot in our tutorials. Parseglob loads up a "glob" of files, this is opposed to using "parsefile" which parses a singular file.

Parse Glob is a littl complicated for one clear reason: Performance vs. Memory.
It's better in terms of performance (we read in ALL of the files and keep them in memory at runtime. It's more expensive memory-wise, but it has us not constantly reloading pages when trying stuff out.)
